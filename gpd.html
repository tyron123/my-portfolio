Graphical Process Designer (GPD)

Legacy to future migration, 4+ years of research

Role: Senior UX Designer · Company: TOPdesk GmbH · Tools: Figma, Miro, BPMN · Status: MVP testing

1. The Call to Adventure

TOPdesk’s ITSM platform was fragmented and siloed… GPD: a unified, intuitive way to design and manage processes, anchored in BPMN.

2. The Challenge

Organizational

  • Stakeholder hesitancy
  • No clear MVP

Technical

  • Legacy monolith
  • Greenfield HTML5/SVG

UX

  • Accessible canvas
  • Simplifying logic

3. The Journey

Research: Affinity maps, workflows, personas, audits → insights: fewer clicks, consistent terms, keyboard nav, automation & AI.

Design: Paper → wireframes → MVP; command bar, shortcuts, accessibility-first; manual vs system tasks; SVG links.

4. The Transformation

MVP Highlights

  • BPMN drag & drop
  • Manual & system tasks
  • Starter workflows
  • API-integrated

What It Solves

  • Breaks silos
  • Clarifies routing
  • Modern interactions
  • Foundation for AI

Proud Of

  • Accessibility depth
  • New paradigm
  • Built on research

5. The Return

What I Learned

  • Cross-team collaboration is key
  • Side-panel behaviors are complex
  • Alignment is as critical as pixels

MVP Trade-offs

  • No multi-language
  • No AI yet
  • Subset of BPMN

What’s Next: Design Hub

  • Workflow Designer
  • Action/Event Manager
  • Service Designer
  • SSP Designer

AI & Analytics

  • Auto-suggest steps
  • Bottleneck analytics
  • NLP authoring
  • Smart validation
